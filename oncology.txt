## Oncology, Prostate Cancer %%
Side effects of radiotherapy in prostate cancer?
- Bladder irritation
- Proctitis
- Impotence
- Diarrhoea
- Dysuria
- Severe perineal cutaneous reaction
- Urethral stricture formation

## Oncology, Prostate Cancer %%
Complications of radical prostatectomy?
- Permanent impotence 90%
- Urinary incontinence
- Rectovesical fistula formation
