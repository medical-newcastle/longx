## Rheumatology, Rheumatoid Arthritis %%
Classes of treatments for Rheumatoid Arthritis?
- Disease Modifying Agents:
-- Methotrexate
-- Leflunomide
- Biologics:
-- TNF-alpha inhibitors: Infliximab, adalimumab, golimumab, certolizumab
-- CTLA-protein: Abatacept
-- IL6 inhibitor: Tocolizumab
-- CD20 MAb: Rituximab
-- JAK kinase inhibitor: Baracitinib, Tofacitinib

## Rheumatology, Rheumatoid Arthritis %%
Side effects of treatments for Rheumatoid Arthritis?
- TNF: lymphoma, demyelination, psoriasis, pneumonitis
- IL6: Neutropaenia, perforated diverticula
- JAK: Transaminitis, shingles, thrombosis

## Rheumatology, Biologics %%
What work up should be done pre-biologic therapy?
- Hepatitis B serology
- Tuberculosis testing
- Hepatitis C testing
- Human Immunodeficiency Virus testing

## Rheumatology, Rheumatoid Arthritis %%
How do we assess the effectiveness of therapy in Rheumatoid Arthritis?
- Disease Activity Score in 28 joints (DAS28)

## Rheumatology, Systemic Lupus Erythematous %%
What are some key questions in the history of S.L.E.?
- Rash
- Oral ulceration
- Proteinuria, red cell casts
- Autoimmune haemolytic anaemia
- Leucopenia
- Miscarriages, thromboses
- Seizures, psychosis

## Rheumatology, Systemic Lupus Erythematous %%
What tests are useful in the assessment of new S.L.E.?
- FBE: anaemia, +ve Coombs, leucopenia, thrombocytopenia
- Clotting: Lupus anticoagulant, anti-cardiolipin, beta-2 microglobulin
- Renal: urine analysis
- CNS: Magnetic Resonance Imaging, lumbar puncture
- Serology: anti-doubleStranded DNA, anti-SSA/SSB, complement, anti-histone, Sm
- Cardiac: Echocardiogram

## Rheumatology, Systemic Lupus Erythematous %%
What are the management principles of S.L.E.?
- Non-steroidal anti-inflammatories & hydroxychloroquine for arthralgias
- Azathioprine & methotrexate for serositis
- Pulsed methylprednisolone & cyclosporin for lupus nephritis
- Warfarin for anti-phospholipid syndrome
- Calcium channel blocker & avoidance for Raynaud's phenomena

## Rheumatology, Systemic Lupus Erythematous %%
What are the long-term planning issues around S.L.E.?
- Pregnancy planning, anti-SSA/SSB
- Anticoagulation
- Sun exposure
- Osteoporosis risk
- Cardiovascular risk: Lipids, HbA1c
- Risk of cerebral lupus
- Mental wellbeing in chronic disease
- Malignancy screening
